 # Ecommerce Sales-Dashboard
Rcommended Structure and Order

1.Project Title

E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

2.Project Headline

Interactive Power BI Dashboard for Analyzing E-Commerce Sales, Profitability, Customer Behavior, and Business Performance.

3.Project Description

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze e-commerce sales data and provide actionable business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), evaluate sales trends, identify top-performing states and product categories, analyze customer purchasing behavior, and understand payment preferences. Dynamic filters allow users to drill down into specific states, quarters, and product categories for deeper analysis.

4.Project Purpose

The objective of this dashboard is to help business managers and decision-makers:

Monitor overall sales and profit performance.
Identify profitable products and sub-categories.
Analyze monthly sales trends.
Track customer purchasing patterns.
Understand regional sales performance.
Evaluate preferred payment methods.
Support data-driven business decisions.

5.Tech Stack

Power BI Desktop.
Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation).
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for KPI calculations.
Data Modeling.
Interactive Visualizations.
Excel/CSV Dataset.
Power BI Slicers & Filters.

6.Data Source

Publicly available E-Commerce Sales Dataset (CSV/Excel)

Dataset contains information such as:

Order Date,State,Customer Name,Category,Sub-Category,Sales Amount,Profit,Quantity,Payment Mode.

7.Dashboard Features

Executive KPI Cards:

Total Sales Amount,Total Profit,Total Quantity Sold,Average Order Value (AOV).

Interactive Filters:

State,Quarter,Product Category.

Sales Analysis:

Sales by State,Monthly Profit Trend,Quantity Distribution by Category.

Customer Insights:

Profit generated by each customer,Top customers identified using Tree Map.

Product Performance:

Profit by Sub-Category,Best and worst performing products.

Payment Analysis:

Distribution of orders by payment mode,COD, UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, EMI.

Interactive Dashboard:

Cross-filtering across all visuals,Dynamic slicers,Responsive layout,Business-friendly interface.

8.Key Highlights

Designed a fully interactive business dashboard.
Created dynamic KPI cards for real-time performance monitoring.
Implemented DAX measures for business metrics.
Used Power Query for data cleaning and transformation.
Built multiple visualization types including:

Bar Charts,Donut Charts,Tree Map,KPI Cards,Column Charts.

Enabled drill-down analysis using slicers.
Presented insights in an easy-to-understand executive dashboard.
Improved business reporting by consolidating multiple metrics into a single dashboard.

9.Business Insights

(Examples):

Maharashtra generated the highest sales among all states.
Clothing contributed the largest share of product quantity sold.
COD was the most preferred payment method.
Printers emerged as the highest-profit sub-category.
Monthly profit declined during May–July before recovering later in the year.
A small group of customers contributed significantly to overall profit, highlighting high-value customers.
